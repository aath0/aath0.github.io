#### Bio
Magdalena holds a PhD in Psychology and an MSc in Psychological Research Methods from Birkbeck, University of London; an MPhil in Cognitive Science from University College London; and a BSc in Psychology from the University of Nicosia, Cyprus. 

Her research in the lab focuses on the functional role of intrinsic neural timescales during wakefulness and sleep, as well as on the neural representations of speech and music using intracranial EEG recordings.
